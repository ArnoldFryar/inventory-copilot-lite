The problem

Spreadsheets don’t catch stockouts

Hours lost to manual triage

Materials managers spend 2–4 hours per cycle sorting ERP exports looking for supply risk. That’s time not spent solving problems.

Stockouts found too late

Active coverage gaps hide in thousand-row exports. By the time someone spots them, production is already impacted.

No repeatable, auditable method

Everyone triages differently. Without fixed rules, results change based on who runs the review, and none of it is auditable.

How it works

From ERP export to triage report in three steps

1

Upload your ERP export

Drop any inventory CSV. OpsCopilot auto-detects your columns: part number, on-hand quantity, daily usage, lead time.

2

Deterministic rules classify every part

Fixed coverage rules assign each row to a status: Active Stockout, Stockout Risk, At Risk, Excess, Dead Stock, or Healthy. Same input, same output, every time.

3

Act on the triage report

Get an executive summary, severity-ranked action panel, leadership narrative, and full detail table, ready for your next materials review.
